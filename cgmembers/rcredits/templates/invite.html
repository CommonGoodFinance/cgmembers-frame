About rCredits

{MEMBER_NAME} has invited you to join rCredits™, a new payment system that gives you incentive rewards and helps your community at the same time. Get rewards on every transaction, when you pay with your rCard™. Merchants save a bundle on credit card fees.

rCredits is a project of Common Good Finance™, a 501(c)(3) nonprofit based in Ashfield, Massachusetts.

rCredits Advantages

For Businesses:

  • Minimal processing fees. Save 2-4% on credit card fees. rCredits system transaction fees are never more than 25 cents. Transactions under $10 are free.
  • Fast, easy transactions. Use a webcam, smartphone, or tablet to scan the customer's rCard. rCredits transactions typically take less time than a credit card transaction. Funds go into your account immediately.
  • Incentive rewards. A 5% rebate reward on payments you make in rCredits and a 10% bonus on whatever you sell.
  • Protection from inflation. Continuous inflation-rate return on your account balance.
  • Short-term credit. rCredits gives you a zero-interest line of credit for easier cash flow.
  • Online advertising. List your products and services free on the rCredits website.
  • Supports "buy local". The whole community has a stake in your success.
  • Easy bookkeeping. Administration time is comparable to managing a second bank account. No changes are required to your payroll system. Common Good Finance provides training for employees.
  • Access to capital*. Apply for zero-interest loans for business expansion or startup.

For Individuals

  • Protection from inflation. Inflation-rate return on your account balance.
  • Easy purchases. Your rCard is all you need, to make purchases in stores. You can also make purchases or send invoices using a web browser, text message, or smartphone.
  • Incentive rewards. A 5% rebate reward on whatever you buy with rCredits and a 10% bonus on your pay. Get 5 cents back even on US Dollar purchases.
  • Zero-interest loans. Everyone gets short-term loans on demand, at no cost.
  • Guaranteed value. Convert your rCredits to US Dollars as needed.
  • Financial security*. The rCredits system is designed to serve as the framework for a Common Good Economy; that is, a new sustainable, democratic funding system that puts people and planet first -- a model easily adopted by individuals and businesses in any community, so everyone can have healthy food, a home, healthcare, satisfying work, and a livable world.

For Communities*

  • More funding for sustainability, culture, and human needs. Communities can issue rCredits as needed, allowing them to flourish economically and socially.
  • We decide together what to finance and what to fund. Communities gather to plan what organizations to support and what local businesses to create or expand. We can make those plans together, knowing we can issue rCredits to fund them – as grants or as zero-interest loans.
  • A democratic process that leads toward consensus. rCredits communities use a combination of democratic processes: town meeting style discussions, voting directly or with personal representatives (one person one vote), ranked choices, instant runoff, and approval voting. The outcome leaves as many people satisfied as possible.
  • Stability. The funding we issue together benefits everyone. It does not increase the wealth of a few at the expense of the many. The system gets more and more stable and sustainable as it grows.
  • We can look after our own and help others. With the power to issue credits that act like money, we can take on that responsibility and be effective in building a Common Good Economy and a society to benefit everyone.

* Longer term advantages will start once the rCredits system has been stable for at least six months.
